About Us

Healthcare Hospital Is Multi-Speciality Hospital Situated In Heart Of Pune City Near Historical Place Shaniwar Wada. The Hospital Is Established With The Mission To Provide Quality Health Services And Facilities For The Community To Promote Wellness, To Relive Suffering And To Restore Health As Speedily, Safely And Humanly As Possible. We Provide Treatment For Blood Cancer, Cosmetic Surgery, Weight Loss Surgery, Cosmetic Height Increase, Joint Replacement, Anti-Aging Treatment At Affordable Cost. Universal Hospital Endeavors To Redefine & Bring Healthcare Within The Reach Of Every Individual At An Affordable Cost. We Aim To Provide A Seamless Healthcare Delivery System Encompassing Tertiary, Secondary And Primary Care. Special Attention Has Been Given To The Design And Aesthetics Of Different Categories Of Rooms For The Patient With Each Spacious Room Being Naturally Well Ventilated And Well Illuminated.
